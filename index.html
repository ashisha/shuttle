Features

  • No configurations needed to kick off
  • Utilizes ssh_config, so you don't have to replicate your ssh configurations
  • Sync your ssh_config across devices using Dropbox (I know what you're thinking, but think IdentityFiles)

    • Configure

    • No new config files, use your beloved ~/.ssh/ssh_config
    • Shuttle For Ubuntu is powered by the #Shuttle comments in your ssh_config
    • Your ssh_config remains intact for your command-line pleasures
